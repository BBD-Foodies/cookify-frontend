Vegetable Stir-Fry

Author: Thato-Tladi

Details

Meal Type: Dinner

Serving Size: 3

Preparation Time: 10 - 15 minutes

Categories: Lunch, Dinner

Dietary Requirements: Vegetarian, Vegan

Required Cookware: Wok, Bowl

Ingredients

  • 200 g Broccoli
  • 150 g Carrots
  • 100 g Bell Peppers
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables.
  2. Heat oil in wok and add vegetables.
  3. Add soy sauce and stir-fry until vegetables are tender.
